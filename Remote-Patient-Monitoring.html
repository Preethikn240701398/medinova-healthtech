Remote Patient Monitoring

IoT-powered real-time monitoring solutions that continuously track patient vitals, enabling proactive care and better chronic disease management.

Continuous Care Beyond Hospital Walls

MediNova’s Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) solution allows healthcare providers to monitor patient health in real time using connected medical devices. This enables early detection of health issues and reduces hospital readmissions.

Ideal for Chronic Disease Management

RPM is especially effective for managing chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, cardiac disorders, and respiratory diseases by tracking vital signs and alerting clinicians to abnormalities.

IoT-Enabled Medical Devices

How Remote Patient Monitoring Works

1. Device Setup

IoT medical devices are provided to the patient.

2. Data Collection

Vitals are captured continuously or periodically.

3. Cloud Processing

Data is securely transmitted and analyzed in real time.

4. Clinical Intervention

Doctors intervene early based on alerts and trends.
