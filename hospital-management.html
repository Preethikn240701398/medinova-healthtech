Hospital Management System

A powerful, integrated digital platform designed to streamline hospital operations, improve patient care, and enhance administrative efficiency.

Smart & Centralized Hospital Operations

MediNova’s Hospital Management System (HMS) is an all-in-one digital solution that automates clinical, administrative, and financial workflows. It connects doctors, nurses, staff, and patients on a single secure platform.

Patient-Centric Digital Experience

Our HMS ensures accurate patient records, quick appointment scheduling, transparent billing, and seamless communication—resulting in improved patient satisfaction and quality of care.

Key Modules & Features

Patient Management

Maintain complete digital patient records including history, diagnoses, prescriptions, lab reports, and discharge summaries.

Appointment Scheduling

Online and offline appointment booking with real-time doctor availability and automated reminders.

Billing & Payments

Automated billing system with insurance integration, invoices, receipts, and financial reporting.

Inventory & Pharmacy

Track medicines, medical equipment, stock levels, expiry dates, and supplier management.

Laboratory Management

Manage lab tests, results, reports, and doctor access through secure digital workflows.

Staff & Doctor Management

Role-based access for doctors, nurses, and administrators, ensuring security and accountability.

Reports & Analytics

Real-time dashboards and analytics for hospital performance, revenue tracking, and patient insights.

Security & Compliance

Data encryption, access control, audit logs, and compliance with healthcare regulations.
